IEEE is an abbreviation for Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers. This is a professional association which was formed in January 1 1963 from the amalgamation of the American Institute of Electrical Engineers as well as the Institute of Radio Engineers. Its corporate office is located in New York city whereas its operation center in Piscataway, New Jersey. This association is purely dedicated to facilitate innovation and technological quality for the benefit of the society.

There are more than 417000 IEEE members in over 160 countries around the world including student members, engineers, scientists as well as allied professionals whose technical interests are mainly in electrical and computer sciences, engineering and related fields.

By encouraging various educational units and professional development hours, IEEE strives to help its members to meet their continuing education requirements and develop products and services based on these efforts.
At the pre-college level, IEEE works with industries, universities and government to raise student's calibre in science, math, engineering and technology.
